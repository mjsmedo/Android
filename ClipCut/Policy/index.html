Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-Jul-2026
App Name: ClipCut
Developer: Medo

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipCut ("the app") handles your information. ClipCut is a video trimming and editing app that works entirely on your device. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy — and your data. ClipCut:

  • Does NOT collect personal data
  • Does NOT access or store your location
  • Does NOT upload your videos, photos, or edits to any server
  • Does NOT share any information with third parties
  • Does NOT use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools
  • Does NOT require an account or sign-in

3. Videos and Files You Choose

To edit your videos, ClipCut lets you pick videos from your device using the system file picker, and it saves the videos and screenshots you export back to your device's storage (for example, your gallery). All of this processing happens locally on your device. Your media is never sent to us or to any third party.

4. Notifications

With your permission, ClipCut may show a notification to let you know when a video export has finished. You can allow or deny this when prompted, and change it at any time in your device's app settings. Notifications are used only for this purpose and do not collect any personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • This app does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to remove such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
We recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes.
