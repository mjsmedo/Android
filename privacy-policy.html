CallTally — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 July 2026

CallTally ("the app") is a call-cost calculator for Android. This policy explains what data the app can access and what happens to it. The summary is simple: your data never leaves your device.

Data the app accesses

Call log (optional). If you choose to use the "fill from last call" or "calculate past calls" features, the app asks for the READ_CALL_LOG permission and reads the duration and date of your outgoing calls. It does not read phone numbers, contact names, or call recordings. If you never use these features, the call log is never accessed.

Data the app stores

Your saved rates, calculation history, budget, and preferences are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

Data the app shares

None. CallTally contains no networking code. It cannot and does not transmit any data off your device. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no user accounts.

The only way data leaves the app is when you explicitly use the Export feature, which hands a file to an app of your choosing through the Android share sheet.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data at all.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes any of the above, this page will be updated and the effective date revised before that version is released.
